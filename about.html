Our Story

About Money
and Meaning

We started with a simple question: why does almost every finance publication treat money as the end goal rather than the means to one?

Money and Meaning was founded on the conviction that financial intelligence and personal philosophy aren't separate pursuits — they're the same one. The most sophisticated investors we know aren't just good with numbers. They're unusually clear about what they're building toward, and why.

We launched in 2018 with a single weekly essay. Today, we publish long-form writing on investing, economics, business, and the psychology of wealth for 52,000 monthly readers who want to think more clearly about money — and live more intentionally because of it.

No clickbait. No "10 stocks to buy now." No sponsored content dressed as editorial. No financial advice masquerading as journalism. Just honest, rigorous, readable writing from people who think deeply about these questions for a living.

Editorial Standards

What We Believe

📐
Depth Over Volume

One well-researched essay is worth more than ten quick takes. We publish less and mean more. Quality over cadence, every time.

🔒
Editorial Independence

We accept no advertising, sponsored content, or affiliate arrangements that could compromise our editorial judgment. Our only obligation is to the reader.

🔭
Context, Not Just Data

Numbers without narrative are noise. We explain why markets move, not just that they did. The "why" is where actual insight lives.

💭
Finance Is Personal

Every financial decision is also a values decision. We write about both dimensions honestly — the spreadsheet and the philosophy behind it.
