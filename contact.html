Common Questions

Before You Write

Do you accept unsolicited article submissions?

Yes, carefully. We commission most of our content from our existing team, but we do consider pitches from qualified writers. Please read several of our articles before pitching to understand our voice and standards.

Do you run sponsored content or native advertising?

No. We accept no paid placements, affiliate links, or sponsored posts of any kind. Our editorial independence is non-negotiable. We are reader-funded only.

Can I republish or translate your articles?

Please reach out before republishing. We may grant permission for non-commercial use with full attribution. Translation requests are considered case-by-case.

Is any of your content financial advice?

No. All content on Money and Meaning is editorial and educational in nature. Nothing published here constitutes personalised financial, investment, or legal advice. Always consult a qualified professional.
